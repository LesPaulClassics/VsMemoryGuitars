Test your memory by playing the classic card matching game with 24 pair of guitar cards.  A total of 36 classic electric guitars are included, of which 24 are randomly chosen per game.  Upon the player successfully matching a pair of cards, a full size image of that guitar is displayed along with a short history.

There are three ways to play:
1) One Player
2) One Player Vs The Computer
3) Two Player
In the "Vs The Computer" mode, the computers IQ can be adjusted for varying levels of difficulty.
